History

v0.0.3, 1/12/2017: New brush size setting to adjust brush dimensions on large screens. Streamlined the navigation. Optimized the selection of brushes. Improved the interface on Nexus 5 and 6 devices.

v0.0.2, 5/22/2016: New mask and fill combinations that expanded the brush selection. Designed a sublime color palette library.

v0.0.1, 3/13/2015: SpriteBrush was made as a fun toy for all human Earthlings.
